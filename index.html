About the band

Glass Animals are a British band whose music is in the art pop and indie genres. Its band members are Dave Bayley, Drew MacFarlane, Ed Irwin-Singer and Joe Swaward. Full stuio albums released are Zaba, How to Be a Human Being and Dreamland, with additional EPs and singles. Glass Animals has been together since 2010 and toured internationally. The bands most recent tour, which was supposed to be over summer 2020, was delayed in an effort to not distract from the Black Lives Matter movement. Information from Wikipedia.
